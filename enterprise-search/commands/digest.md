# Digest

Runs the **daily-digest** skill: a grouped catch-up on what changed across your tools.

## Instructions
1. Read the time window from the argument (e.g. "since Friday"). If empty, default to since the last digest or the past 24 hours.
2. Invoke the **daily-digest** skill for that window.
3. Sweep connected sources for what's new or changed, filter to what's relevant to the user, and group by topic.
4. Lead with a "top 3 things to know," then the grouped items with links, then mentions and asks.
5. Offer to open any item with **unified-search** or synthesize a hot topic with **knowledge-synthesis**.
