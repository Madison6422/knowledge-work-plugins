# Product Management
Write feature specs, plan roadmaps, and synthesize user research faster. Keep stakeholders updated and stay ahead of the competitive landscape.

## What's inside

### Skills
| Skill | What it does |
|-------|--------------|
| `write-spec` | Drafts a spec from a rough idea — problem, goals, non-goals, user stories, requirements, metrics, open questions. |
| `roadmap-plan` | Builds or updates a roadmap grouped by theme and quarter, with effort/impact, dependencies, and capacity risks. |
| `research-synthesis` | Turns interviews, surveys, and support signals into themes, insights, and ranked bets. |
| `stakeholder-update` | Writes a concise update — what shipped, next, risks, asks — tailored for exec or team. |
| `competitive-landscape` | Tracks competitor moves, launches, positioning gaps, and implications. |

### Commands
| Command | What it does |
|---------|--------------|
| `/product-management:write-spec` | Runs `write-spec` on a feature or problem. |
| `/product-management:roadmap` | Runs `roadmap-plan` for a quarter or theme. |
| `/product-management:stakeholder-update` | Runs `stakeholder-update` for a given audience. |

## Customize for your team
- Replace `<!-- CUSTOMIZE -->` marks in each skill with your terminology, scoring model, and metrics.
- Set your **priority scale** and **north-star metrics** in `write-spec`.
- Set **themes**, **scoring model**, and **team capacity** in `roadmap-plan`.
- Define your **competitor set** and **differentiators** in `competitive-landscape`.
- Point connectors at the right boards, channels, and spaces in your tools.
