# Plan Campaign

Runs the **campaign-plan** skill to turn a goal into an executable, channel-by-channel plan.

## Instructions
1. Read `$ARGUMENTS` for the campaign goal and any constraints (deadline, budget, target number).
2. Run the **campaign-plan** skill: pull messaging from `~~knowledge base`, segments from `~~email marketing`, and draft dated tasks in `~~project tracker` when connected.
3. Assemble audience, channels and messaging, timeline, asset needs, and KPIs with a baseline and target.
4. Present the plan for human approval before any task is scheduled or send is queued — confirm budget, owners, and dates first.
