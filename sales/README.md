# Sales

Prospect, craft outreach, and build deal strategy faster. Prep for calls, manage your pipeline, and write personalized messaging that moves deals forward.

## What's inside

### Skills
| Skill | What it does |
| --- | --- |
| `account-research` | Deep-dive a target company before first contact — what they do, size, recent news/funding/leadership, likely pains, and who to talk to. |
| `call-prep` | Prepare for a specific call — account snapshot, attendee research, prior-touch history, suggested agenda, discovery questions, and likely objections. |
| `draft-outreach` | Write a personalized outreach sequence grounded in real account context. You approve before anything sends. |
| `pipeline-review` | Review open opportunities — stage, next step, risk flags, and a prioritized action list. |
| `competitive-battlecard` | Build a battlecard for a competitor — positioning, strengths, weaknesses, landmines, and objection handling. |

### Commands
| Command | What it does |
| --- | --- |
| `/sales:call-prep` | Runs **call-prep** for a company or meeting. |
| `/sales:pipeline-review` | Runs **pipeline-review** for a rep, segment, or your deals. |
| `/sales:draft-outreach` | Runs **draft-outreach** for a prospect or account. |

## Customize for your team
- Add your product summary, ICP, and the pains you solve so research and outreach map to real value.
- Set your sales stages, discovery framework, and risk thresholds in the relevant skills.
- Paste your best-performing emails and approved competitive messaging as style references.
- Define who must approve before any outreach sequence loads into a cadence.
- Look for `<!-- CUSTOMIZE: ... -->` marks in each skill — those are the spots to edit.
